Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1/2cup popcorn kernels
  • 2-3 tbsp oil (vegetables,coconut,or any natural oil)
  • 2 tbsp butter (optional, for flavor)
  • Salt to taste

Process to make Popcorns

  • heat the oil
  • wait for popping
  • sake it up
  • listen for pops
  • add butter and salt
  • Serve and enjoy!

Optional flavors

  • Cheese Popcorn
  • Caramel Popcorn
  • Masala Popcorn
