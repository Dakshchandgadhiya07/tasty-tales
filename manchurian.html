Ingredients

For Manchurian Balls

  • 1 cup cabbage,finely shredded
  • 1/2cup carrot,finely grated
  • 1/4cup capsicum,finely chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp spring onions (optional)
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour(maida)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • oil for deep frying or shallow frying

For the Sauce(Gravy)

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger
  • 2 tbsp spring onion whites
  • 1/2onion,finely chopped
  • 1/2capsicum,chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornflour+1/4cup water
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • spring onion greens for garnish

Process to make manchurians

  • make the Manchurian Balls
  • Prepare the Gravy
  • Combine
  • Serve hot with fried rice,noodles enjot it!
