Ingredients

For The Pizza dough

  • cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp suger
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For The Pizza sauce

  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup or puree
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • Salt to taste

Toppings

  • Mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Bell peppers (sliced)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (sliced)
  • olives (optional)
  • Sweet corn
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • oregano & chili flakes (for garnish)
