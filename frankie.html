Ingredients

For the filling

  • 2 medium potatoes,boiled & mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • \
  • 1 small capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1/4cup grated carrot
  • 1/4tsp turmeric
  • 1/2tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2tsp garam masala or frankie masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Coriander Leaves,chopped
  • Salt to taste

For Roti / Wrap

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • salt & water to knead
  • oil or butter for roasting

For Assembly

  • Green chutney or mayonnaise
  • tomato ketchup
  • sliced onions
  • chaat masala
