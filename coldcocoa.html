Cold Cocoa

Main Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp suger
  • 1/4cup hot water
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2-3 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate ice-cream
  • chocolate syrup

Process to make Cold-cocoa

  • Make a Cocoa syrup
  • blend it
  • pour into a tall glass
  • add a scoop of ice-cream on top if using
  • drizzle some chocolate syrup on top for garnish
