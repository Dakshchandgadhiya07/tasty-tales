Mint Mojito

Main Ingredients

  • 10-12 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/2lemon (cut into small wedges)
  • 1 cup soda or sparkling water(chilled)
  • ice-cubes (as needed)
  • a pinch of salt (optional)
  • mint sprig & lemon slice (for garnish)

Process to make mint-mojito

  • Muddle the base
  • use a muddler or the back of a spoon to gently crush the mint and lemon-this releases the flavors
  • fill the glass with ice cubes
  • pour chilled soda or sparkling water over the ice
  • top it up
  • Mix Everything Well
  • stir and garnish
  • Garnish with a sprig of mint and a lemon slice

Optional Twist:

  • Add a few drops of jaljeera or a pinch of black salt for a desi-style mint mojito!
