Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 packet noodles (instant or hakka style)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic (optional)
  • 1 small onion,sliced
  • 1 small capsicum,sliced
  • 1 small carrot,juliened
  • 1/2cup cabbage,shredded
  • 1-2 green chilies,sliced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • spring onions for garnish (optional)

Process to make noodles

  • boil noodles
  • stir fry veggies (onion,garlic,green chilies)
  • add other veggies (carrot,capsicum,cabbage)
  • add sauces (soy souce,ketchup,vinegar,salt,pepper)
  • add noodles
  • garnish & serve
