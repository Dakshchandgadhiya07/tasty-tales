Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 2-3 large potatoes (russet type is best)
  • oil for deep frying (vegetable or sunflower oil)
  • Salt to taste

Optional Seasonings

  • Red chili powder or peprika
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • mixed herbs or oregano
  • peri-peri masala(for spicy fries)

Optional for Extra Crispiness

  • 1-2 tbsp cornflour or rice flour
  • ice-cold water (for soaking potatoas)
