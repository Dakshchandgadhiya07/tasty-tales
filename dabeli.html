Ingredients

For Dabeli Masala

  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 small stick cinnamon
  • 2 cloves
  • 1/2tbsp fennel seeds

For the Potato Filling

  • 2 large Potatoes,boiled and mashed
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp dabeli masala
  • 2 tbsp tamarind-date chutney
  • 2-3 tbsp water
  • salt to taste

For Garnishing/Assembling

  • Pav buns
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2-3 tbsp finely chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
  • nylon sev
  • tamarind chutney & garlic chutney
  • Fresh coriander (optional)
